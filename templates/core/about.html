About RamJaap

Our Mission

RamJaap is dedicated to providing a digital platform for devotees to practice Ram Naam Jaap. Our mission is to make spiritual practice accessible in the digital age, allowing devotees to connect with their faith regardless of location or circumstances.

By providing tools for tracking progress and joining a community of like-minded devotees, we aim to encourage regular spiritual practice and create a supportive environment for personal growth.

What is Ram Naam Jaap?

Ram Naam Jaap is the practice of repeating Lord Ram's name as a form of meditation and devotion. In Hindu tradition, chanting Ram's name is believed to purify the mind and heart, bringing spiritual benefits and inner peace.

The practice involves repeating "Ram" with focus and devotion, either aloud or silently. Many practitioners set targets for the number of repetitions or dedicate specific time periods to this practice daily.

How to Use RamJaap

  1. Create an account or log in to track your progress
  2. Choose a timer option or set a target number of repetitions
  3. Begin typing "Ram" in the input field (any variation like RAM, RaM will be counted)
  4. The counter will automatically track each occurrence of "Ram"
  5. Use the pause feature if you need to take a break
  6. Submit your session when complete
  7. View your statistics and progress on your personal dashboard
